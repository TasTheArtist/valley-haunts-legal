Privacy Policy for Valley Haunts This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and disclosure of Your information when You use Valley Haunts and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You. Data Privacy Summary Personal Information: Valley Haunts does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, or phone number). User Content: All data, selections, or progress entered into the application are stored locally on your device using web storage. This data is not accessible by the developer or any third party. Push Notifications: Valley Haunts does not send push notifications. All interactions occur locally within the application while it is in use. Company (referred to as either "the Company", "We", "Us" or "Our") refers to Valley Haunts. Device means any device that can access the Service such as a computer, a cell phone, or a digital tablet. Service refers to the Application. You means the individual accessing or using the Service. Collecting and Using Your Personal Data Types of Data Collected Valley Haunts does not collect Personal Data. Because the application operates entirely locally on your device, we do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect information such as your name, email, or physical location. Usage Data As an offline-first application, Valley Haunts does not automatically collect or transmit diagnostic data, IP addresses, or browser metadata to our servers. Use of Your Data Since we do not collect your data, we do not use it for marketing, profiling, or data analysis. Any data you enter into the app is used solely to provide the app’s functionality on your own device. Delete Your Personal Data You have the right to delete your data at any time. Because your data is stored locally on your device: You can clear the app's cache/data in your device settings. You can uninstall the Application, which will remove all locally stored data. Children's Privacy Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. Because no data is transmitted to our servers, we do not "process" children's data. How to Exercise Your Rights: To submit a privacy request, please contact us via email at developer@tastheartist.com. We will respond to all verified requests within 45 days.
